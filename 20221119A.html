Powerlessness

Here's a fun story: Recently, I scheduled four days off from work so I could spend some time remixing and mastering some songs. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to do what I'd planned, though, because the power went out for nearly four days. Strange coincidence, don't you think? Is the world trying to tell me something? Either way, I've decided that I really do need to get out of here, so I applied for another two jobs in another state. Each job sounds pretty good, actually, so I hope I can land one. I'm pretty disillusioned with this situation, and I'm definitely ready for a change.
