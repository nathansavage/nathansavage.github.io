Leaving Something Behind

I'm an old man. I live alone on a small island off the coast of the mainland, unfortunately. Recently, I abruptly ended a longtime friendship because I became tired of it. In my free time I make music in hopes of leaving behind something worthy of being remembered. If you think it would be interesting to see a film about someone in a similar situation, I recommend you watch The Banshees of Inisherin. I'm not exactly like the character Colm in the film, obviously. For example, I don't have a dog, I don't smoke, a civil war isn't currently being fought in my country (let's wait and see what happens next) and I haven't started cutting off my own fingers—yet.
