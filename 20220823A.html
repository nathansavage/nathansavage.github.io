Forgetting To Eat

It hasn't happened in a long time, but recently I became so engaged with what I was doing that I forgot to eat. In the past, I'd often forget to eat while working on some complicated computer program, for example, but recently I forgot to eat while I was working on music for about nine hours. Good times.
